## Who Are We?

**Centrala Termică Service** is a specialized company in repairs, maintenance and installation of heating systems. Founded with the mission to bring comfort and safety to clients in Bucharest and Ilfov, we have become a trusted partner in the field.

### Experience and Expertise

With **over 10 years of experience** in the field, our team has developed deep expertise in working with premium brands such as Ariston, Viessmann, Ferroli and Motan. Each technician is certified and benefits from continuous training to keep up with the latest technologies.

### Our Values

- **Quality** - we use only original and certified parts
- **Transparency** - clear prices, no hidden costs
- **Promptness** - we respect the deadlines we commit to
- **Safety** - we work with maximum safety standards
- **Satisfaction** - our client is always at the center

### Our Team

Our team consists of **expert technicians**, passionate about what they do and involved in every project. Each team member has:
- Official certifications for the brands we work with
- Practical experience of over 5 years
- Continuous training in the field
- Professional attitude and impeccable service

### Our Services

We specialize in:

1. **Emergency Repairs** - 24/7 availability
2. **Preventive Maintenance** - annual service programs
3. **Installations and Replacements** - modern solutions for comfort
4. **Technical Consultation** - audits and recommendations

### Coverage Area

We cover **all of Bucharest** and **Ilfov county**, providing quality services at our clients' homes. We schedule interventions quickly, depending on your availability.
