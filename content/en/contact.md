### Where Are We?

**Ilfov:** Voluntari, Bragadiru, Pantelimon, Popești-Leordeni, Chiajna, Buftea, Măgurele, Berceni, and other localities.

**Note:** For on-site interventions, we schedule based on technician availability and urgency level.

### What Happens After You Contact Us?

1. **Receiving your request** - we respond within 2 hours during business hours, immediately in case of emergency
2. **Scheduling** - we agree on a convenient time by phone
3. **Intervention** - the technician arrives at the scheduled time, performs diagnosis and provides a clear price
4. **Work completion** - if you agree, we fix the problem
5. **Billing** - after completion, you receive the invoice by email

**Need urgent assistance?**  
Call the emergency number directly for the fastest intervention!
